Tracker Usage:
  • Make sure the tracker is configured exactly how you want it before beginning. Changing some settings after marking off items may not update certain parts of the tracker correctly.
  • Left clicking on any object toggles whether or not they are collected.
  • Left clicking on a key or upgrade (boots/hammer) increments the counter, right clicking will decrement the counter.
  • Star Spirits will highlight when all the requirements to acquire them glitchlessly are met.
  • Right clicking on a Star Spirit will increment a number from 1-7, that can be useful for tracking chapter difficulty with the "shuffle chapters" option in seeds.
  • Right clicking on various "optional" items will add a checkmark to them, this can be used to signify that they have been turned in.
  • Settings are expected to be configured before items are marked. Some items or completion states may not update when changing settings.
  • This site uses localstorage to remember your previously used settings! Some browsers or plugins may disable this when you disable cookies as well. This won't break anything, but you will have to manually re-enter your settings each time.
  • If something looks weird, try force refreshing the page (Shift+F5 in Windows browsers for example).
  • For suggestions and feature requests, please make issues on Github! If you don't have a Github, you can also reach out on any of the social media in the footer.
  • This is just a tracker, to generate a seed and play yourself, head over to https://pm64randomizer.com/
